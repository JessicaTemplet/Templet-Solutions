About Jessica Templet, JD

**Jessica Templet, JD**, is the founder of **Templet Solutions LLC** and the creator of the **C⁴AT³ Framework**—the first systematic methodology for **AI Citation Optimization (AICO)**.

Her work bridges the gap between content science and search systems, defining how AI models evaluate **credibility, actionability, and technical excellence** in digital material.

A former legal analyst turned AI strategist, Jessica built Templet Solutions to answer a question no one else was asking: **What makes AI systems trust one source over another?**

Her research has since shaped measurable standards for **AI-recognized authority**, uniting structured data, content architecture, and behavioral validation into one verifiable process.

Jessica’s expertise extends beyond theory. She has led structured optimization programs across industries—applying the **C⁴AT³, TEACH, and CITE frameworks** to elevate clients into top-tier visibility across AI-driven search and generative platforms such as **ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini**.

She is known for treating SEO as an **empirical science** rather than an algorithmic guessing game—combining schema design, causal testing, and narrative precision to make content provable rather than merely persuasive.

Through Templet Solutions, Jessica continues to refine how **authority, trust, and truth** are operationalized in the age of AI search—turning what was once an art of intuition into a discipline of measurable intelligence.

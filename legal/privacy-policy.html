Templet Solutions Privacy Policy

This policy details how Templet Solutions collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide to us.

Last Updated: October 4, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Templet Solutions** ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide to us solely for the purpose of receiving email communications and facilitating service consultation. We are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our subscribers and visitors. We will only use your personal information to send you the content you requested and to manage necessary business functions (scheduling, payments).

2. Data We Collect

When you subscribe to our email list or consult with us, we collect the following **Personal Data**:

  • **Email Address**: Mandatory for receiving email communications.
  • **Name, Address, Phone Number**: Collected if you are retained for services (e.g., consultation, scheduling, and billing purposes).
  • **Technical Data**: Our email service provider (MailerLite) automatically collects data such as your IP address, subscription source, time of subscription, and engagement data (e.g., whether you opened an email or clicked a link).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected Personal Data for the following specific purposes:

Purpose of Use Legal Basis (GDPR)
Email Communications Consent
To send you the weekly news letters and special offers that you explicitly subscribed to.
Service Provision Contractual Necessity
To schedule consultations, confirm appointments, and facilitate billing for contracted services.
System Integrity Legitimate Interest
To maintain our subscriber list, filter out spam, and verify your consent (proof of opt-in).
Service Improvement Legitimate Interest
To analyze engagement and improve the quality and relevance of our communications.

What Is AI Citation Optimization (AICO)? — The Complete Guide

January 20, 2025 | By Jessica Templet

TL;DR:

AI systems don't care about your effort—they care about trust, structure, and utility. AICO is the practice of designing content so ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity cite you as authoritative. While SEO targets rankings, AICO targets citations. When AI cites your content, it validates your authority and amplifies reach through every conversation that references your work.

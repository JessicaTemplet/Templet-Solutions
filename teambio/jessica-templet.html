Pioneering AI Citation Optimization (AICO)

The rise of Generative AI and conversational search fundamentally changed SEO. The simple goal of ranking #1 was replaced by the need to be the source that AI systems like Google's Gemini, Perplexity, or Anthropic's Claude **cite as authoritative fact**. Recognizing this shift early on, I developed the **C³AT Framework** (Credibility, Context, Connection, Actionability, Technical Excellence).

The C³AT Framework is a proprietary system designed specifically to make content inherently citation-worthy. This isn't traditional SEO; it's a strategic process of content engineering that focuses on the signals AI models look for when synthesizing answers. I work with clients to ensure their intellectual property is not just read by people, but is formally referenced by the very systems that are rewriting search itself.

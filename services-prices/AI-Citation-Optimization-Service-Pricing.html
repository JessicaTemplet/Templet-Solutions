Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I start with an audit and then move to a retainer?
Yes. Most clients begin with the C³AT Framework Content Audit to understand their current state and opportunities, then move to monthly retainer services for ongoing optimization.
Q: What if I need more content optimized than my package includes?
Additional content can be added at $400-600 per piece depending on complexity and length. Or you can simply upgrade to the next service tier.
Q: Do you write content or just optimize existing content?
We primarily optimize existing content, but we offer Done-For-You content creation as an add-on service for clients who need C³AT-optimized content written from scratch.
Q: How quickly will I see results?
Initial AI citation improvements typically appear within 4-8 weeks. Significant authority building through content clusters takes 4-6 months of consistent implementation.
Q: What makes your approach different from traditional SEO services?
Traditional SEO optimizes for search engine rankings. AICO using the C³AT Framework optimizes specifically for AI system citations in conversational responses. These require different optimization strategies, though they complement each other.
Q: Do you guarantee specific citation numbers?
No. AI citation frequency depends on many factors including topic competition, content quality, and AI platform algorithm changes. We guarantee systematic implementation of proven C³AT principles, not specific citation counts.
